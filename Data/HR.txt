The company follows 9 working hours per day for all employees.
Office timing is from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.

Employees are entitled to 18 paid leaves annually.
Leaves must be approved by the reporting manager in advance.

Late arrival beyond 15 minutes will be marked as half-day if repeated frequently.

Work from home is allowed only with manager approval and valid reason.

Employees must follow the company code of conduct and maintain professional behavior.

Any violation of company policies may lead to disciplinary action or termination.

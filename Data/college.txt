1. College Admission FAQ Dataset
Admission Process

Students can apply online through the college admission portal. They must submit personal details, academic records, and required documents. Applications are reviewed by the admission team before confirmation.

Documents Required

Students must provide:

10th Marksheet
12th Marksheet
Passport-size photographs
Aadhaar Card
Transfer Certificate
Fee Payment

Fees can be paid online using UPI, debit card, credit card, or net banking. Students may also pay at the institute office.

Attendance Policy

Students must maintain at least 75% attendance to be eligible for examinations.

Batch Timing

Morning Batch: 9 AM – 1 PM

Evening Batch: 2 PM – 6 PM

Refund Policy

Application fees are non-refundable. Tuition fee refunds are processed according to institute guidelines.
